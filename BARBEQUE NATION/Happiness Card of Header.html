How to Reedem
1. Selection
The Barbeque Nation Happiness
Card comes in multiple
denominations. Please select the one
that fits your requirement and
budget.
2. Payment Mode
It’s extremely simple to buy the
Happiness Card that you desire. We
have multiple payment models
available on the app: you can choose
to pay through your Debit/Credit
Card, Netbanking, UPI Transfers or
payment wallets.
3. Voucher Details
Once your payment is successful, the
voucher details will reflect on your
profile automatically. You can use the
voucher code the next time you make
your reservation/booking through our
app.
4. Make Payment
In order to make your table booking,
you will have to make the payment in
advance. While doing so, you can use
the voucher code or the Happiness
Card that you have purchased.
