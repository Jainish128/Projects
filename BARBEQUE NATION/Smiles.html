Smiles Coins Club

Earn and redeem Smiles Coins for every celebration! Grill and enjoy lovely meals while earning Smiles Coins where each Smiles Coins earned is a rupee!
This loyalty program is here to reward you and put a smile on your face.

SMILES COINS

What is the Smiles Coins program all about?
Smiles Coins is a loyalty program to treat all our customers! It is complimentary and allows you to earn smiles every time you dine with Barbeque Nation. You can redeem these smiles
on your next visit to any of our outlets.
How do I join Smiles Coins Club?
All Barbeque Nation customers are auto-enrolled into the Smiles Coins program. You just need to download our app or login to www.Barbequenation.com using your mobile number to
know further about your Smiles Coins!
How do I earn Smiles Coins?
Managing Smiles Coins is easy as 1,2,3. Reserve a table with the same number you used to create an account for Barbeque Nation. Enjoy a lovely meal with delicious grills, main course
and desserts. Pay your bill as you like, either online through our app or at the outlet and you will automatically be credited with Smiles Coins in your account. These smiles can be
redeemed on your next visit.
How do I earn Smiles Coins?
You will earn 5% of the billing amount before taxes and they will be auto credited to you. Smiles Coins earned can be redeemed on your next visit. Each Smiles Coins is equivalent to a
rupee! That means 500 Smiles Coins = 500 rupees!
What is the value of each Smiles Coins at the time of redemption?
Each Smiles Coins is worth a rupee. That means 500 Smiles Coins = 500 rupees!
Where can I get information about my Smiles Coins?
Tracking your Smiles Coins is a piece of cake. Just log in to Barbeque Nation via app or website, go to your profile and click on Smiles Coins. This page will inform you about the amount
of Smiles Coins earned, redeemed and even expired.
How do I redeem my Smiles Coins?
To redeem your Smiles Coins, log in to Barbeque Nation through your mobile app or website and book a table. During check out, you will find an option to add Smiles Coins and reserve
a table.
How do I exchange my unused Smiles Coins?
We ensure to inform you close to when Smiles Coins so that you can utilize them. However, if you are unable to utilize them you have an option to use those Smiles Coins and buy our
Happiness Card, which has a 6 month validity from the date of purchase.
